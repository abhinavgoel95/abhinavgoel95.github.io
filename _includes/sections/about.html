About

I am an experienced Research Assistant working in the area of energy-efficient artificial intelligence (AI) and computer vision (CV). With a strong background in programming, I use my knowledge in computer engineering to find novel techniques to make AI algorithms more deployable on edge devices.

The ability to perform large scale computing on the cloud is fantastic. However, as the amount of data that needs to processed increases - the performance of these massive, centralized compute systems will soon get throttled by the laws of physics. Real-time AI applications can not wait for data to be beamed out to large data centers, located miles away. As data-collecting sensors become more pervasive and far-reaching - a truly autonomous and intelligent future needs AI computation to migrate to the edge.
